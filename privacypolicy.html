These two apps are an unofficial application of Bangladesh Railway, offering features such as train schedules, train information, station details, train tracking, coach details, route information via Google Maps, station-wise train listings, key station contact information, and railway officers' phone numbers and emails. The information is primarily sourced from the Bangladesh Railway's official website: https://railway.portal.gov.bd and is presented in an easy-to-use format for our users.

Additionally, the app facilitates registration and login on the official Bangladesh Railway e-ticket website, ticket purchasing, ticket verification, profile management, and ticket history access through the Bangladesh Railway e-ticket system: https://eticket.railway.gov.bd.

These apps are designed to simplify the user experience by aggregating and presenting all relevant information in a convenient way. We gather schedule, train, and station data from the official Bangladesh Railway website and supplement this with additional information—such as station latitude and longitude—sourced from Wikipedia and other resources. After collecting the data, we integrate and present it in a manner that enhances usability, which is why our app is highly rated by users.

Please note, these apps are developed independently and are not affiliated, associated, authorized, or endorsed by any government entity or railway corporation. While we aim to provide accurate and up-to-date information, users are encouraged to verify details such as schedules and ticket availability through official channels. The app serves as a convenient platform relying on publicly available data, but any purchases made through it are subject to the terms and conditions of the official railway service. The developers are not liable for any discrepancies, errors, or issues that may arise from the use of information or services provided by the app.
